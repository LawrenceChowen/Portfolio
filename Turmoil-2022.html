Turmoil - Short Film (2022)

Turmoil - Short Film (2022)

Throughout the start of 2022 I have been working on the short film Turmoil directed by Kuba Liśkiewicz. Throughout the production process I worked closely with producer Lusia Oates and cinematographer Mantas Kazlauskas.

For this project I took on the role of Assistant Director. This involved the creation of pre-production documents such as call sheets, alongside a great deal of logistical management for the production. I was further tasked with going beyond the scope of Assistant Director, as to be expected with most smaller scale productions.
I have listed a few skills I managed to utilise alongside my time working on Turmoil:

➢ Due to the smaller crew and budget I was tasked with helping in the replanning and distribution of the schedule. As to support our producer who unfortunately obtained covid prior to shooting. This furthermore required me to fill in the gaps where possible for the physical absence of the producer - fortunately Lusia still managed to oversee production via mobile communication (through teleconferencing applications such as zoom)

➢ To support the camera department I was asked to help set up c-stands and lighting equipment. Which was unconventional for an assistant director.

➢ Due to the smaller scale crew and budget we had to optimise our time accordingly. I therefore made a conscious effort to insure that the crew was well informed of the shoots logistical elements (schedule changes, personnel changes, etc.) prior to shooting in an effort to minimise the chance of miscommunication. Adversely, I made a key effort to keep pressure off of the director as not to detract from the crews creative efforts. Finding a balance between informing the director of hurdles, whilst also keeping stress levels down is one of the hardest elements of being assistant director - I however felt I managed to get the balance right on this production.
