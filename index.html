Services

I often find people asking what services a content producer provides. Outside of understanding the production, producers often pickup the slack and keep any given production on track. Whether its meeting deadlines or saving money, producers are a necessary component to production. Please find below some of my key services.

Content Production

I have directed and produced my own media in tandem with industry regulation. I have produced hours of hand crafted content from my home lab and have successfully produced pilots, shorts and commercial content without studio support.

Production Technician

Whether I am needed for a single shoot or for studio maintenance. I am a trustworthy professional who can get the best use out of specialist camera equipment, from: cameras and lenses to tripods and motion sensors.

Video Editing and Grading

A hard working video editor with a time efficient and result focused ethic. I edit on: Premiere Pro, Avid and Davinci Resolve. Capable of producing LUTs or a draft colour grade depending on the needs of production.

Creative Direction

A confident and thorough creative with a passion for captivating and deep storytelling. Within my own creative sphere, I tend to prefer science fiction epics with gritty twists; More in the vein of Bioware’s Mass Effect or Ridley Scott’s Blade Runner. I can help drive, inspire and ultimately bring a production to life.

Production Management

Depending on the demands of your production, I am open to producing a wide range of production resources, including: the creation and operation of live schedules, the writing of industry standard legislation or monitoring production to assure industry regulations are met.

Backend Asset Management

A trained digital imagery technician with good understanding of virtual asset storage, storage solutions and delivery of virtual assets. Specialising in .ARRIRAW and .R3D file formats.
