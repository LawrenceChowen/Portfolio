Star Wars the Old Republic - Unmasked

Content Creation - Star Wars™ the Old Republic 

Throughout my film career I have had the opportunity to work alongside the Bioware™ corporation, I was given the honor of becoming an official Star Wars the Old Republic™ content creator. I have since made hundreds of hours worth of documentaries, trailers and community videos for the Star Wars community (2016 - 2024).

To conclude my time as a Bioware Content Creator I created a three hour long documentary on Star Wars the Old Republic titled "Unmasked" that covered Star Wars the Old Republic's decade long history. Throughout the years I have also produced countless docu-shorts, entertainment videos and interactive discussions for members of the Bioware community.

As an independent project, I set out to demonstrate my ability to both handle all aspects of production and further yet self motivate without incentives. The long form video took a year to develop and involved the unique creation of hundreds of virtual assets. Needing throughout scriptwriting, integrated fact checking and a charismatic charm to accurately portray Bioware’s development of Star Wars the Old Republic.

The documentary went on to receive critical acclaim for its accuracy and positive outlook on Bioware's development. Please find below links to both articles.
