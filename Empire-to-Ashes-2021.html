Empire to Ashes - Commercial Shoot (2021)

Music Videos - Empire to Ashes During the second quarter of 2021 I produced and oversaw the production of two music videos as well as a selection of promotional photographs for the metal band “Empire to Ashes’ whereas I coordinated the movement and acquisitions of all shoot assets. Working alongside director Sam Harlow, I obtained the necessary capital and assets needed to fulfil the director's vision.

S. Harlow, Taking Back Control (2021)
S. Harlow Me Myself and I (2021)

Whilst working on this project I had to work closely with the band, in an effort to understand their own personal style and bespoke needs concerning the music video. We needed to therefore use specialist cameras and equipment to get the most out of the production.

➢ Specialist Equipment - working with black-light, and powerful cameras with a higher dynamic range (HDR) to record in almost complete darkness.

➢ Collaboration - working in a professional manner to satisfy all parties including the directors and the band, this required me to mediate decisions and ultimately fulfill the creative vision. Our use of creative solutions such as black light, really made for a powerful production that satisfied all parties.

➢ Production Management - when working on the music video I had to coordinate multiple elements of the production, including: Crew/equipment travel, location availability, band schedules and other miscellaneous services. All to assure that both shoots happened in a manageable and professional way.
