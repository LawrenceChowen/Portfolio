The Strength Tribe - Commercial Shoot (2022)

The Strength Tribe - Commercial Shoot (2022)

Leading up to the final quarter of 2021, I worked alongside director Gonçalo Conceição to produce a promotional video the The Strength Tribe, a commercial fitness company. My role for this project was producer, production manager and assistant director. I was tasked with creating this promotional video without the support of a production house. Meaning all equipment, forms and legal would have to be acquired indepdently.

➢ Equipment and Production Rentals - for this project I was tasked with acquiring all the various productions elements indepdently. I therefor has to enquire about equipment set ups, lights, utilities and props from various manufactures to obtain the best price.

➢ Legislation and Bureaucracy - as I was not associated with a specific production house (beyond the directors freelance insurance), I had to write up and structure the official legislation myself- resulting in the creation of dozens of legal contracts, used for the production. I further produced numerous forms, call sheets and agreements for use alongside the production; this includes forms for both catering and travel.

➢ Production Management - whilst working on the project I adopted the production manager position. This required that I create adequate schedules and info sheets to best manage the flow of production.

I found this production to be highly engaging on a practical level. Not only was I required to research every element of production, due to the small crew size. But I also handled the creation and delivery of a dozen legal forms, which needed constant tweaking and drafting to assure the productions legal safety. I finally managed the day-to-day production to solidify its fluidity, and I can ultimately say the shoot was a relaxing, yet stressful success! As producer I was able to assure that the team comprised of cast and crew I enjoyed working with, making the production all-the-more motivating.
